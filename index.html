Offline Reading

Save newsletters with a single click. Jitter downloads the raw HTML and caches it securely in your browser's IndexedDB, meaning you can read your favorite publishers on the subway or an airplane.

100% Open Source

Jitter believes in transparency. Powered by WebRTC (PeerJS), the network connects clients directly to the server hub without invasive third-party tracking. The code is yours to verify and modify.

Are you a Publisher?

Jitter provides a distraction-free environment for your readers. No algorithms, no spam filters, just pure content delivered via direct publisher codes.

Publisher codes are exactly 6 digits long. Distribute them anywhere.
